Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Community Volunteer Platform. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data such as your name, email, phone number, and other relevant information when you register or participate in volunteering activities.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to connect you with volunteering opportunities, improve our services, and communicate with you regarding platform updates.

4. Data Protection

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except when required by law or for the purpose of providing volunteering services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Contact us if you wish to make changes or request data removal.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@volunteerplatform.com.
